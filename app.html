Blueprint Hub + QuickBooks � Better Together
Blueprint Hub manages your leads, jobs, crew, and day-to-day operations. QuickBooks handles your accounting, payroll, and taxes. Export your data here to keep both in sync � no double entry.
?? Invoice Export
Export invoices as a CSV formatted for QuickBooks import. Filter by date range.
?? Customer Export
Export your clients as a QuickBooks Customer List CSV. Import once to skip re-entering everyone.
?? How to Import into QuickBooks
1
Export from Blueprint Hub
Click the Download button above. A .csv file will save to your Downloads folder.
2
Open QuickBooks
Log into your QuickBooks Online or Desktop account.
3
Go to Import
In QuickBooks: Settings ? Import Data ? Invoices (or Customers).
4
Select Your File
Upload the .csv from your Downloads folder. QuickBooks will map the columns automatically.
